Coming Soon...

Hard to believe but it’s been almost 3 years since I was regularly posting comics online. When I ended my last comic, I really expected to take a couple months off and get right back at it. To be fair to myself, I did make about 6 or 7 attempts to start a new one, so I really have been working on something for most of that time. But for various reasons, none of them ever really stuck. I just couldn’t seem to find one that really got me excited to put in the time that it takes to do something like this. Or I would get super excited and then something about it just didn’t feel right and since I had about a million other ideas, I would just move on. I have a lot of pages for some of these unfinished projects that will probably never see daylight. That’s probably for the best, though. I will say, I’ve learned a lot over the past couple years even though it seems like I don’t have anything to show for it.

Anyway, the point to these ramblings is that in the near future I’m going to be launching a new comic. I don’t have an exact date yet, but when things become a little more clear I’ll be sure to bug the hell out of everyone on the interwebs that doesn’t mute/unfollow/ban me. You can follow along here or on twitter if you want to know more, and I apologize in advance for the spamming I’m gonna do (not really).
